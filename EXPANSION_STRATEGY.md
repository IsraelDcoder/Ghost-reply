## 🎯 CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### Do These or You Fail

1. **Nail Dating First**
   - Get to $1M-5M ARR dating revenue
   - THEN expand to other verticals
   - Don't dilute focus

2. **Data is Your Moat**
   - Every reply = training data
   - Better model than competitors
   - Use conversations to improve AI

3. **Speed > Perfection**
   - Launch features in 2-3 weeks, not 3 months
   - Competitors will copy fast
   - First-mover wins

4. **Retention > Acquisition**
   - Get 1 user to $100+ LTV before scaling
   - 80% of revenue comes from retention
   - Churn kills you

5. **Build Community**
   - Social features
   - Leaderboards
   - Share best replies
   - Make it viral

6. **Never Stop Improving AI**
   - Your moat is AI quality
   - Train on real conversations
   - Use feedback to retrain

## 🚨 DANGEROUS MISTAKES (Don't Do These)

1. **Trying to do everything at once**
   - Focus on dating for first 6 months
   - Expand after proving PMF

2. **Ignoring retention**
   - Easy to get users
   - Hard to keep them
   - 5% churn = death
   - Track this obsessively

3. **Not building community**
   - B2C apps need social features
   - Sharing = viral growth
   - Implement this from day 1

4. **Pricing too low**
   - $4.99 is good starting point
   - But test $9.99, $14.99 tiers early
   - Professional users will pay $50+/mo

5. **Forgetting B2B**
   - B2B ARR > B2C ARR usually
   - Sales team, 18-month contracts
   - Start B2B sales in Month 6-12

6. **Bad AI quality**
   - Replies need to be GOOD
   - Or users churn immediately
   - Always be improving model
